# oscar

Email reminders and rescheduling for GP appointments.

## Brief

More than 15 million GP appointments are missed annually, costing the NHS hundreds of millions of pounds. Your task is to build a proactive, ethical appointments assistant for a GP surgery that will call or email remind the patients prior to their appointments to confirm date and time, and increase the likelihood the patient will attend. There should be a good conversational flow, with the assistant leading the conversation, and learning over time how to handle conversations more effectively and even become personalised based on patientsâ€™ input. Extra features include handling prescription renewal, and appointment booking by phone or email.
